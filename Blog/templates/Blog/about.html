About.
Everything you need to know about Corkran.

Some write. Even fewer want others to see. And a lot less write something worthy of being seen. That last part is something that may as well be ignored. Here, is where you can put just about anything you want up for everyone to see, provided that's what you want. Or perhaps you just want to write, or maybe read what others have written. It's a free land, this. Do what you will. Here, what you write is who you are.

This blog was created, and is maintained entirely by one person. What this means, that if you've got any improvements, suggestions, or new features you'd like to see (that aren't completely muddle-headed), you can email me about the same.

For those of you who care, we don't collect any information about you that you don't know about, and we don't sell your information at all. You have total control over what you share with us, and that also brings us to a small disclaimer - the user-uploaded content on this site reflects the views, and opinions of that user only. We have nothing to do with it, apart from putting it up on the website. That's a slight catch about freedom - we can't be biased, so you may find something you don't agree with, but there's not often much we can do about it; they're only exercising the same rights you have.

You may want to read our official terms and conditions, privacy policy, and content disclaimer. Then again, you may not.
