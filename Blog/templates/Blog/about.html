Everything you need to know about Corkran.

Some write. Even fewer want others to see. And a lot less write something worthy of being seen. That last part is something that may as well be ignored. Here, is where you can put just about anything you want up for everyone to see, provided that's what you want. Or perhaps you just want to write, or maybe read what others have written. It's a free land, this. Do what you will. Here, what you write is who you are. This blog was created, and is maintained entirely by one person. What this means, that if you've got any improvements, suggestions, or new features you'd like to see (that aren't completely muddle-headed), you can email me about the same.
